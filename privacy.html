What we collect

  • Account: your email address (used for sign-in codes and nothing else).
  • Your app data: watchlists, alerts, paper trades and simulator state you choose to sync to your account.
  • Community chat: messages you post and direct messages you send, stored to deliver the feature.
  • Push tokens: a device token if you enable notifications.
  • Operational logs: request paths and status codes (no query strings) for reliability.

What we do not collect

No trading credentials are stored beyond a broker token you explicitly connect; no location, contacts, camera or microphone access; no advertising identifiers; no sale of data to anyone.

Your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Access & correction: your synced data is visible in the app; write to us for a copy.
  • Erasure: Account → Delete account removes your account and every server-stored document immediately.
  • Consent withdrawal: deleting the account withdraws consent; the app keeps working without an account.

Retention & security

Account documents are kept while the account exists and deleted on account deletion. Sessions are signed cookies; data in transit is encrypted once the service operates behind TLS (see the deployment status page). Data is hosted in a single-region server.
