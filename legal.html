Disclaimer & Privacy

Not investment advice

TaurEye is a research and screening tool. Everything it shows — signals, scores, backtests, probability models, comparison reports, relationship graphs and news — is a factual or statistical composite of public market data, presented for research and education. Nothing in this app is a recommendation to buy, sell or hold any security. Do your own research and/or consult a SEBI-registered investment adviser before acting.

Data accuracy

  • Market data comes from public sources (NSE, Yahoo Finance, screener.in, niftyindices.com, public RSS feeds) and may be delayed, incomplete or wrong. Always verify against your broker or the exchange.
  • Relationship graphs labelled AI-generated are produced from an AI model's general knowledge, not verified regulatory filings. Treat them as indicative starting points for research.
  • Backtests are hypothetical, ignore some real-world costs, and past performance does not predict future results.

Privacy

  • Your watchlists, portfolio entries, track list, filters and settings are stored only on your device (browser localStorage / app storage). The server never sees them.
  • The server keeps short-lived caches of public market data and standard web-server logs (IP, path, timestamp) for reliability and abuse prevention. No accounts, no tracking pixels, no analytics, no ads.
  • News links open third-party sites governed by their own policies.
